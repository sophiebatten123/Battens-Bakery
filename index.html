Batten's Bakery is a small family ran buissness who puts its customers at the heart of all of its recipes. Founded in 2018 the company is now one of the most popular bakeries in Borrowash, with clients boasting about the incredile FRESH pasteries and the DELICIOUS molton chocolate cakes.
Our staff ensure that every bake is as good as the last and believe in delivering 100% everytime. Our unique service allows you to dine in or dine out our simply bake recipes from the comfort of your own home.

So why not join us today for a bakery experience like no other. A bakery experience which puts you in charge and gives you only the best cakes and pasteries. As soon as you walk in the bakery doors you will be overwhelmed with the smell of chocolate, pasteries and sweets.
